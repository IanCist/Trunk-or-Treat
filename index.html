Welcome to Marlboro High School Trunk or Treat Event!

Date: October 29th (Rain Date: October 30th)

Time: 4:00 PM - 5:30 PM

Location: Marlboro High School Parking Lot

This event will feature over 30 trunks, music, fun activities, and snacks for sale! A free event for kids ages 12 and under. All proceeds will go to benefit the American Cancer Society.

Highlights:

  • Wear a Halloween costume!
  • Snacks & refreshments available for purchase.
  • A Nut-Free Event!
  • Proceeds benefit the American Cancer Society.
  • Face Painting.
  • Feds on the Go and Goldy's Ice Cream will be present for refreshments!

For more info, contact Kmcgeough@frhsd.com.
